We Get It
Finding a Trustworthy Breeder Shouldn't Be This Hard

Choosing a new puppy is a big decision — and the internet hasn't made it easier. Between puppy scams, puppy mills, and breeders who won't let you visit, it's hard to know who to trust.

⚠️

Online Scams Are Everywhere

Fake listings, stolen photos, and breeders who disappear after payment — you deserve better.

😟

You Can't See What You're Getting

Most breeders ship sight-unseen. You want to meet the puppy and the family that raised it.

❓

No Support After the Sale

Many breeders vanish once the transaction is done. You need ongoing advice and support.

We Don't Just Sell Puppies.
We Match Families.

We believe that every home deserves a loving companion, and our story begins with that very thought. My name is Ron Hess, and for many years, I've dedicated my life to connecting families like yours with their perfect Doodle puppy.

"It wasn't just my passion for dogs that led me down this path, but a pivotal moment from my childhood — a time when my dad brought home a dog that never quite fit with our family. The disappointment we all felt fueled my determination to ensure that no one else had to go through such heartache."

Common Questions
Everything You Need to Know

We specialize in Doodle breeds including Goldendoodles, Labradoodles, and Bernedoodles. All puppies are raised in our home and socialized with our family from birth. Contact us for current and upcoming litter availability.

Every puppy comes with a written health guarantee covering genetic and congenital conditions. All puppies receive vet checkups, age-appropriate vaccinations, and deworming before going home.

Absolutely — we encourage it! We welcome families to visit our home in Russell, MA. For those who can't visit in person, we also offer Zoom meetings so you can see the puppies and ask questions.

We do not ship puppies. We focus on New England families and local adoption. You can pick up at our home in Russell, MA, or we can arrange breeder delivery within a reasonable distance.

Pricing varies by breed and litter. Contact us for current pricing — we're happy to discuss openly. The price includes vet checkups, vaccinations, deworming, a health guarantee, and a starter kit.

Your puppy comes with vet records, vaccination history, health guarantee, a starter supply of their food, and a blanket with their mother's scent. Plus Ron's personal number for lifelong support.
